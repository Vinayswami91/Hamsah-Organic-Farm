What to do at Hamsah

Come picking

Enjoy some time on the farm, picking fresh delicious organic fruit and vegetables. We have 2 types of Mangoes, Chickoos, Pomegranates, and a lot more. There can be no better way to spend time on a weekend

Learn Sustainable Practices

We compost and have adpoted perma culture practices. Our huts are completely made of natural material. We have set up a watershed. The benches in the kitchen are made from compacted mud. Much more can be said, but is better seen.

Help out on the weekend

We are constantly working to make the farm a better place for everyone. There is always room for help if people want to. What's needed can range from helping build the huts, weeding, tilling, cleaning up the place, picking, and having a good time.

What you can find here

This is much more than a plot of land where fruit and vegetables grow.

We're working to make this a wonderland for all. It's quite tough to find a spot in the farm without a view. Have a look at some of them
